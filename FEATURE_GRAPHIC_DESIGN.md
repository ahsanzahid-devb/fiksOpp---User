## Typography

**App Name:**
- Font: Bold, Sans-serif (e.g., Roboto Bold, Montserrat Bold)
- Size: 72-96px
- Color: White or dark (high contrast)

**Tagline:**
- Font: Regular or Medium, Sans-serif
- Size: 24-32px
- Color: White or light gray

**Features:**
- Font: Regular, Sans-serif
- Size: 18-24px
- Color: White or light

## Design Tools

You can create this using:
1. **Canva** (free, easy)
   - Template: "Google Play Feature Graphic"
   - Size: 1024 x 500
   - Add your logo, text, and icons

2. **Figma** (free, professional)
   - Create custom design
   - Export as PNG

3. **Photoshop/GIMP** (advanced)
   - Full control over design

4. **Online Tools:**
   - Placeit.net
   - AppMockup.com

---

## Step-by-Step: Create in Canva

1. Go to [canva.com](https://canva.com)
2. Create custom size: 1024 x 500 pixels
3. Add background (gradient or solid color)
4. Add your app logo (left or center)
5. Add text "FiksOpp" (large, bold)
6. Add tagline "Your Trusted Service Booking Platform"
7. Add feature icons and text (right side or bottom)
8. Export as PNG (high quality)
9. Verify size is exactly 1024 x 500

## Final Notes

- Keep it simple and clear
- Don't overcrowd with too much text
- Use high-quality images/icons
- Ensure text is readable
- Test how it looks on different devices
- Follow Google Play design guidelines
