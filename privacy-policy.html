1. Introduction

FiksOpp ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application FiksOpp (the "App").

2.1 Personal Information

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name and contact information (email address, phone number)
  • Profile information (profile picture, address, location)
  • Payment information (processed securely through third-party payment processors)
  • Account credentials (email, password - stored securely)

2.2 Service Information

  • Job postings and service requests
  • Booking history and preferences
  • Messages and communications with service providers
  • Reviews and ratings

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (device type, operating system, unique device identifiers)
  • Usage data (features used, time spent, pages viewed)
  • Location information (when you enable location services to find nearby providers)
  • Log data (IP address, access times, app features accessed)

2.4 Third-Party Information

  • Information from social media accounts (if you sign in with Google or Apple)
  • Information from payment processors (transaction details)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process transactions and send related information
  • Send you technical notices, updates, and support messages
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and requests
  • Communicate with you about services, offers, and promotions
  • Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities
  • Personalize and improve your experience
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues
  • Facilitate communication between users and service providers
  • Verify your identity and prevent fraud

4. How We Share Your Information

4.1 Service Providers

We may share your information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, including:

  • Payment processors (Stripe, Razorpay, PayPal, PhonePe, etc.)
  • Cloud storage providers (Firebase/Google Cloud)

  • Customer support services

4.2 With Other Users

  • Your profile information (name, photo, ratings) is visible to service providers
  • Your job postings are visible to service providers
  • Your reviews and ratings are publicly visible

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

5. Camera and Photo Information

We request camera permission to:

  • Allow you to take profile pictures
  • Upload service-related images

Camera access is optional, and you can deny this permission. Photos you take are stored securely and only used for app functionality. You can delete photos you upload at any time.

6. Location Information

We collect location information to:

You can control location sharing through your device settings. Note that disabling location services may limit certain features of the App.
